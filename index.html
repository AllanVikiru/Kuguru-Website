Who is Kuguru Limited?

Kuguru Food Complex Limited was founded in 1988. It is one of the top manufactures of beverages in the Kenyan Republic. It is a family business founded by the family patriarch, Mr. Peter Kuguru. He and his wife built up the company for five years before being joined by four new family members.

Softa Bottling Company Ltd (SBCL) manages several great brands. Softa and Babito soft drinks maintain international standards through technical backing from Dohler Euro Citrus in Germany.
